Projects

Here's an overview of some of the things I do with my time.

Physics

ABRACADABRA: I'm currently working with Professor Lindley Winslow's group on ABRACADABRA, a cryogenic search for axion cold dark matter. I'm working on characterizing parts of the current detector system as well as performing calculations for an upcoming new version of the detector.

BAND: Prior to this, I worked with Professor Or Hen's group on BAND, a neutron detector at Jefferson Lab in Newport News, VA collecting data to better understand the behavior of nucleons in short-range correlated pairs. I worked on developing a laser calibration system for the detector, measuring and calculating relevant parameters of the detector's photomultipliers and scintillator bars, and writing calibration software for data collection. I was also stationed at Jefferson Lab for the summer of 2018 working on BAND's assembly, installation, and setup. I presented a poster on my work at the 2018 APS Division of Nuclear Physics yearly meeting in Waikoloa, Hawaii.

Physics community: I'm an officer of MIT's Society of Physics Students. I've helped organize the Greater Boston Undergraduate Physics Conference, various faculty talks and lunches, career workshops, and outreach efforts to local schools. Outside of SPS, I've been an 8.03 tutor and an 8.044 grader. I'm also teaching a class on black holes for this year's Spark.

Design

HackMIT: I'm on the organizing committee for HackMIT, MIT's premier hackathon and one of the largest undergraduate hackathons in the country. I directed the marketing team for HackMIT 2018 and developed the event's branding and image while running a 7-person team. I also worked on marketing and logistics for Blueprint, a hackathon for high schoolers run by our team. I designed materials for Blueprint 2018 and Blueprint 2019.

MIT Shakespeare Ensemble: I have been the publicity designer for the MIT Shakespeare Ensemble's past five shows: Rumors, Richard III, She Kills Monsters, Queen Lear, and Go Back for Murder. I've designed posters and digital and print advertisements for each show.
